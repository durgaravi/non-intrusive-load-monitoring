Locality Statistics | See how your locality contributes to saving electricity

This graph shows your electricity consumption along with your neighbours for the previous month.

What is this?

These statistics show how your locality helps reduce power consumption. This data gives an idea to the government officials as to how much power needs to be distributed to your locality. This also helps keep in check any power leakage.
